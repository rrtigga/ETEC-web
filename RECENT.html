We are The Engineering and Technology Entrepreneurship Club at UC Davis.

Founded in 2012, E-TEC’s aim is to provide a space for engineering, science, and technology students at UC Davis to learn more about technology entrepreneurship, become aware of university and national resources available for student entrepreneurs, exchange ideas, form teams that can participate in the entrepreneurship programs on campus organized by UC Davis, and ultimately help create more student startups.

OUR GOAL:

We provide a community for entrepreneurial-minded students who share an interest in creative innovation - a productive and collaborative environment for business people, developers, and everyone in between to connect and share their talents.

GET YOUR HANDS DIRTY!

Our quarterly events educate UC Davis students to bring their startup ideas to life with industry expert guidance, mentorship, and support.

 

Grow and Learn

Every week, we organize workshops led by professionals to introduce students to concepts such as management responsiblity and raising startup capital. The ESSC features a 3D printer/scanner, a computer monitor, whiteboard walls and a Shopbot CNC device for milling and machining plastic, wood and aluminum. This is the perfect opportunity to hone your skills and develop your product! Teams will have the privilege to work in exclusive co-working spaces meant for them to work on their startups.
